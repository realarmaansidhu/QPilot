## SEC Publisher's Exclusion Basis

QPilot operates under the publisher's exclusion (Section 202(a)(11)(D) of the
Investment Advisers Act of 1940):

1. **Impersonal analysis** — Same output for all users given the same ticker
2. **No money management** — We never handle user funds or execute trades
3. **General circulation** — Content is available to all subscribers equally
4. **Algorithmic generation** — Scores are computed, not manually curated
5. **No individual tailoring** — No consideration of personal financial situations

## Precedent

The 2024 Seeking Alpha court ruling confirmed that AI-generated buy/sell ratings
are protected under the publisher's exclusion when they meet the above criteria.
